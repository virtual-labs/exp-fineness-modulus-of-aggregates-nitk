Fineness modulus of fine aggregates

Objective

To determine the fineness modulus and fine size distribution of fine aggregates (river sand and beach sand) by sieve analysis.

Apparatus used:

Mechanical shaker, Trays, Set of fine sieves with pan, Weighing scale etc.

 Description Fineness Modulus of Fine Aggregate is an imperial factor obtained by adding the cumulative percentages of fine aggregates retained on each of the standard sieves and dividing the sum by an arbitrary number 100.
